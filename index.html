About

Senior Journalist, Media Strategist & Social Reformer

Dr. Rahmat Kanchagar (PhD) is a senior journalist, media strategist, social reformer and entrepreneur with more than 25 years of experience across journalism, public service and corporate leadership.

As MD & CEO of Manikya Money Service Pvt Ltd, he has built a career around creating meaningful social impact through responsible media, business innovation and community development. A PhD holder in Journalism, he is widely respected for fearless reporting, a people-centric approach and a sustained commitment to social welfare.

Over the years he has produced and broadcast more than 1,000 impactful stories across Karnataka, using journalism as a tool to bring real change to communities. That same instinct — identifying a need on the ground and mobilising resources to meet it — now shapes how he leads a multi-sector enterprise, where each venture is built to serve a clear public or commercial purpose rather than to chase scale alone.

His work bridges two worlds that rarely meet: the discipline and public accountability of the newsroom, and the long-horizon thinking of an entrepreneur. It is this combination that defines his leadership style — informed by lived reporting experience, anchored in social responsibility, and oriented toward building institutions that outlast any single story.

Among the first generation of electronic media professionals in Karnataka, Dr. Rahmat Kanchagar's career has spanned Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Delhi, Ahmedabad, Chennai, Goa and Belagavi — a reporting footprint that gave him a rare, on-the-ground view of how the country actually changes.

He is especially known for his courageous field coverage during the 2008 Mumbai 26/11 attacks, and for his political analysis during major Indian elections. Across both, the through-line is the same: being present where it matters, reporting without fear, and translating events into something the public can understand and act on.

That reporting foundation continues to inform his role as a media strategist and entrepreneur today, shaping ventures that treat information, access and community as things worth building carefully.
